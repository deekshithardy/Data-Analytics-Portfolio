# Business Insights

## Key Findings

- Technology is the highest revenue-generating category.
- Some categories generate high sales but comparatively lower profit.
- The West region contributes the highest overall sales.
- Certain states consistently outperform others in revenue.
- Some sub-categories show negative or very low profit despite good sales.
- Average transaction value helps understand customer purchasing behavior.
- High discounts do not always lead to higher profitability.

## Business Recommendations

- Increase inventory for top-performing categories.
- Review pricing strategies for low-profit products.
- Focus marketing campaigns on high-performing regions.
- Reduce excessive discounts on low-margin products.
- Improve sales strategies for underperforming categories.
